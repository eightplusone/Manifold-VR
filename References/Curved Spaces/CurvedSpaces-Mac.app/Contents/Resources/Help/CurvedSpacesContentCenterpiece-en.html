Centerpiece

The Earth and the galaxy look the prettiest, while the Earth and the gyroscope best illustrate the relative orientations of neighboring cells. For example, with the Earth or the gyroscope you can easily tell whether neighboring cells differ by a reflection or a 180° rotation. The galaxy is the Whirlpool Galaxy; the image is adapted from an image captured by the Hubble Space Telescope.
