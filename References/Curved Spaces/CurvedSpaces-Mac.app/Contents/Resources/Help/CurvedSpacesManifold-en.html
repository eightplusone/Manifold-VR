How to change the space

Choose Space ▸ Change Space… and select any file in the Sample Spaces folder. The Basic sub-folder contains a few particularly simple and beautiful spaces to start with. The Spherical, Flat and Hyperbolic sub-folders contain a richer variety of spaces, grouped by curvature.

Experts may wish to create their own spaces.
