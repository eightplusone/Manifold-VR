  • The book The Shape of Space introduces finite universes to advanced high school students and beginning university students. Suitable for self-study as well as classroom use. Available from its page at Amazon.com.
  • The Torus and Klein Bottle Games let children of all ages have fun while building their intuition for finite universes in two dimensions. Available for free online.
